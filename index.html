Design note for TraceWeave

I designed the DPP as an operational scroll experience, not a regulatory archive. The main decisions were to make readiness visible first, group the nine DPP blocks into a narrative flow, and use confidence/evidence signals so the Sustainability Manager knows exactly what needs action. For the certification layer, I applied a KYC-style model: every claim has evidence, verifier, date, expiry and confidence level, but the interface only surfaces the friction when it changes a business decision.

Prototype scope: fictional brand and product data for the TraceWeave practical case. This is a UX prototype, not a legal compliance opinion or a production DPP data model.

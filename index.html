Certifications & Evidence

Each sustainability claim is tied to evidence, certifier, verification date, confidence level and expiry. This applies onboarding-grade evidentiary rigor without turning the UX into an audit archive.

High confidence

Third-party certificate, valid date, product or batch match, and known certifying body.

Medium confidence

Supplier declaration plus partial third-party evidence, or valid evidence not fully mapped to batch UID.

Low confidence

Self-declaration only, expired document, unclear certifier, or missing product/batch link.
